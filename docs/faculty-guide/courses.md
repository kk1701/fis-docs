# Courses Taught

The Courses Taught tab lets you record the courses you have taught or are currently teaching. Each entry links to a course from the central course catalog maintained by the admin.

---

## Adding a Course Record

1. Click **+ Add Course**
2. Fill in the details:

| Field | Description | Required |
|---|---|---|
| Course | Select from the course catalog dropdown | Yes |
| Role | Lecturer / Coordinator / Lab | Yes |
| Semester | ODD / EVEN | Yes |
| Academic Year | e.g. 2024-25 | Yes |
| Hours/Week | Number of teaching hours per week | No |
| Mode | Theory / Lab | No |
| Notes | Any additional notes about this course | No |

3. Click **Add**

:::info
Courses must exist in the central catalog before you can add them to your teaching history. If a course you taught is not in the dropdown, ask your admin to add it to the catalog.
:::

---

## Course Roles

| Role | Description |
|---|---|
| Lecturer | Primary instructor for the course |
| Coordinator | Course coordinator managing multiple sections |
| Lab | Laboratory instructor |

---

## Filtering Your Course Records

Use the filters at the top of the tab to narrow down your teaching history:

- **Semester** — filter by ODD or EVEN semester
- **Academic Year** — type an academic year (e.g. 2024-25) to filter by year

Filters apply immediately without needing to click a search button.

---

## Editing a Course Record

Click **Edit** on any course record to open the form pre-filled with existing data. You can update the semester, academic year, role, hours, mode, and notes. The course itself cannot be changed — delete the record and create a new one if you selected the wrong course.

---

## Deleting a Course Record

Click **Delete** on any course record. You will be asked to confirm before the record is removed.

---

## What is Displayed

Each course record shows:

- Course name and code
- Semester and academic year
- Your role
- Hours per week (if provided)
- Mode — Theory or Lab (if provided)
- Notes (if provided)

---

## Relationship to Analytics

Course records feed into two analytics reports:

- **Course Load Analysis** — uses `hoursPerWeek` to rank faculty by teaching load and identify single-faculty courses
- **Department Health Score** — counts total courses taught as one of the health metrics

Course records are also visible on your public faculty profile under the **Courses** tab, showing your complete teaching history to the public.
