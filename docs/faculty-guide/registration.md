# Registration & Approval

This page explains how a faculty member creates an account and gets approved to access the full dashboard.

---

## Registering an Account

Faculty accounts are created through the self-registration form at `/register`. No admin action is needed to create the account — only to approve it.

### Steps

1. Go to your institution's FIS URL and click **Register** on the landing page
2. Fill in the registration form:
   - **Full Name** — your name as it should appear on your profile
   - **Email** — your institutional email address. This is your login credential
   - **Department** — select your department from the dropdown
   - **Password** — minimum 6 characters
   - **Confirm Password** — must match the password field
3. Click **Create account**

After submitting, your account is created with `PENDING` status and you are redirected to the faculty dashboard.

:::info
The department dropdown is populated from the catalog managed by the admin. If your department is not listed, contact your administrator.
:::

---

## After Registration — Pending State

Immediately after registration, your account is in `PENDING` status. During this time:

- You can log in
- You can see the dashboard layout and tab headers
- All tabs are disabled and a pending banner is shown
- You **cannot** edit any profile information

---

## The Approval Process

Your registration appears in the admin's **Approvals** section. The admin will either approve or reject your account.

### If Approved

- Your account status changes to `APPROVED`
- All dashboard tabs become active
- You can now fill in and edit all sections of your profile

### If Rejected

- Your account status changes to `REJECTED`
- You can still log in but cannot edit your profile
- Contact your administrator to understand the reason and next steps

---

## Logging In

Go to `/login` and enter your registered email and password. After a successful login you are redirected to the faculty dashboard.

---

## Forgot Password

If you forget your password:

1. Click **Forgot password?** on the login page
2. Enter your registered email address
3. A 6-digit OTP is sent to your email — valid for 10 minutes
4. Enter the OTP on the verification page
5. Set your new password

See the [Security](../security) section for full details.

---

## Changing Your Password

Once logged in, you can change your password at any time:

1. Click your name in the top navbar
2. Select **Change Password** from the dropdown
3. Enter your current password and your new password
4. Click **Change Password**
