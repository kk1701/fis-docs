# Thesis & Dissertations

The Thesis & Dissertations tab lets you record your research supervision activity — PhD thesis supervisions and PG dissertation supervisions. Each has its own sub-tab.

---

## Sub-tabs

| Sub-tab | Description |
|---|---|
| Thesis Supervisions | PhD thesis supervision records |
| Dissertation Supervisions | PG (Master's level) dissertation supervision records |

---

## Adding a Thesis Supervision

1. Select the **Thesis Supervisions** sub-tab
2. Click **+ Add Record**
3. Fill in the details:

| Field | Description | Required |
|---|---|---|
| Thesis Title | Full title of the thesis | Yes |
| Research Area | Primary research area, e.g. Machine Learning, VLSI Design | Yes |
| Year | Year of submission or award | Yes |
| Status | Ongoing / Completed / Submitted / Awarded | Yes |
| Your Role | Supervisor / Co-Supervisor / External Examiner | Yes |

4. Click **Add**

---

## Adding a Dissertation Supervision

1. Select the **Dissertation Supervisions** sub-tab
2. Click **+ Add Record**
3. Fill in the details:

| Field | Description | Required |
|---|---|---|
| Dissertation Title | Full title of the dissertation | Yes |
| Specialization | Specialization area, e.g. Data Science, Power Systems | Yes |
| Year | Year of completion | Yes |
| Your Role | Supervisor / Co-Supervisor / External Examiner | Yes |

4. Click **Add**

---

## Supervision Roles

| Role | Description |
|---|---|
| Supervisor | Primary guide for the research |
| Co-Supervisor | Joint guide alongside a primary supervisor |
| External Examiner | External evaluator for the thesis or dissertation |

---

## Thesis Status Values

| Status | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Ongoing | Research is currently in progress |
| Submitted | Thesis has been submitted for evaluation |
| Completed | Evaluation complete, result awaited |
| Awarded | Degree has been formally awarded |

Status badges are color-coded:
- 🟢 Green — Completed or Awarded
- 🟠 Orange — Ongoing
- 🔵 Blue — Submitted

---

## Editing a Record

Click **Edit** on any supervision record. The form opens pre-filled with existing data. Make your changes and click **Update**.

---

## Deleting a Record

Click **Delete** on any supervision record. You will be asked to confirm before the record is removed.

---

## What is Displayed

### Thesis record
- Title
- Research area
- Year
- Role badge
- Status badge (color-coded)

### Dissertation record
- Title
- Specialization
- Year
- Role badge

---

## Relationship to Analytics

Supervision records feed into the **Supervision Pipeline** analytics report, which shows:

- How many theses are ongoing, submitted, completed, or awarded per department
- Top supervisors by total supervision count
- Active supervision load per faculty

Thesis supervisions are also shown on your public faculty profile if you have any records — displayed under the **Thesis** tab on your public page.
