# Academic Profile

The Academic Profile tab stores your current academic position and qualifications at the institution.

---

## Fields

| Field | Description | Required |
|---|---|---|
| Designation | Your current title, e.g. Assistant Professor, Associate Professor, Professor | No |
| Highest Qualification | Your highest academic degree, e.g. PhD, M.Tech | No |
| Department | Your department. Can be updated here if it has changed since registration | No |
| Joining Date | The date you joined this institution | No |
| Years of Experience | Total years of academic or professional experience | No |
| Specialization | Your research and teaching specializations — comma separated, e.g. Machine Learning, Data Science | No |

---

## Specialization

The specialization field accepts multiple values separated by commas. These are stored as an array and used in:

- The faculty directory — specialization tags appear on your public faculty card
- Analytics — the Research Domain Profiling report uses specializations to group faculty by domain
- Public profile — all your specializations are displayed as tags

**Example input:**
```
Machine Learning, Computer Vision, Deep Learning
```

This will appear as three separate tags on your profile and in the directory.

---

## Saving

Fill in the fields you want to update and click **Save Changes**. A green success message confirms the save.

---

## Relationship to Other Sections

The academic profile feeds into several other parts of the application:

- **Designation** appears on your faculty card in the public directory
- **Specialization** appears as tags on your directory card and public profile
- **Department** determines which department you appear under in the directory and analytics reports
- **Years of Experience** is used in the Department Health Score analytics report
- **Highest Qualification** is used in the Qualification Distribution analytics report and displayed on your public profile
