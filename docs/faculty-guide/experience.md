# Experience

The Experience tab lets you record your professional experience across four categories. Each category has its own sub-tab within the Experience section.

---

## Experience Categories

| Category | Description |
|---|---|
| Teaching | Academic teaching positions at universities or colleges |
| Industrial | Work experience in the industry or private sector |
| Research | Research positions, fellowships, or projects |
| Administrative | Administrative or management roles within academic institutions |

---

## Adding an Experience Entry

1. Select the appropriate sub-tab (Teaching, Industrial, Research, or Administrative)
2. Click **+ Add Experience**
3. Fill in the details:

| Field | Description | Required |
|---|---|---|
| Title / Designation | Your job title or designation, e.g. Assistant Professor, Software Engineer | Yes |
| Organization / Institution | Name of the employer or institution | Yes |
| Start Date | When you started this position | Yes |
| End Date | When you left this position. Leave blank if this is your current position | No |
| Location | City and state of the position, e.g. Bhopal, MP | No |
| Pay Scale | Pay level or scale, e.g. Level 10 | No |
| Details / Nature of Work | Brief description of your responsibilities | No |

4. Click **Add**

---

## Current Positions

If you are currently in a position, leave the **End Date** field blank. The system will display it as **Present** in the timeline.

---

## Switching Between Categories

Click the category pill buttons (Teaching, Industrial, Research, Administrative) to switch between experience types. The form and list update to show only that category's records.

When you click **+ Add Experience**, the form defaults to the currently selected category. The category is set automatically — you do not need to select it in the form.

---

## Editing an Experience Entry

Click **Edit** on any experience entry. The form opens pre-filled with the existing data. Make your changes and click **Update**.

---

## Deleting an Experience Entry

Click **Delete** on any experience entry. You will be asked to confirm before the record is removed.

---

## What is Displayed

Each experience entry shows:

- Title / Designation
- Organization name
- Date range (start → end or start → Present)
- Location (if provided)
- Pay scale badge (if provided)
- Nature of work / details (if provided)

---

## Relationship to Analytics

Experience records feed into two analytics reports:

- **Experience Profile** — counts how many faculty have teaching-only vs multi-domain experience per department
- **Department Health Score** — average years of experience is one of the weighted metrics in the department health calculation

Experience records are also shown on your public faculty profile under the **Experience** tab, grouped by type.
