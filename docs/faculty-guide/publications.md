# Publications

The Publications tab lets you record your research output across four publication types. Each type has its own sub-tab.

---

## Publication Types

| Type | Description |
|---|---|
| Journal | Articles published in academic journals |
| Conference | Papers presented and published at conferences |
| Book | Books authored or co-authored |
| Book Chapter | Chapters contributed to edited books |

---

## Adding a Publication

1. Select the appropriate sub-tab (Journal, Conference, Book, or Book Chapter)
2. Click **+ Add Publication**
3. Fill in the details. Fields vary slightly by publication type:

### Common Fields (all types)

| Field | Description | Required |
|---|---|---|
| Title | Full title of the publication | Yes |
| Authors | Comma-separated list of all authors, e.g. Dr. John Doe, Dr. Jane Smith | Yes |
| Year | Year of publication | Yes |
| Pages | Page range, e.g. 123-135 | No |
| URL | Link to the publication online | No |
| Citation Text | Full citation string | No |

### Journal and Conference only

| Field | Description | Required |
|---|---|---|
| Journal Name / Conference Name | Name of the journal or conference | No |
| DOI | Digital Object Identifier, e.g. 10.1109/... | No |
| Indexing | SCI / Scopus / None | No |

### Book and Book Chapter only

| Field | Description | Required |
|---|---|---|
| Publisher | Publisher name, e.g. Springer, Wiley | No |

4. Click **Add**

---

## Indexing

The indexing field is available for Journal and Conference publications only. It accepts three values:

| Value | Meaning |
|---|---|
| SCI | Science Citation Index — highest impact |
| Scopus | Scopus indexed |
| None | Not indexed in SCI or Scopus |

Indexing is used in the **Publication Trends** and **Faculty Research Momentum** analytics reports. SCI publications are weighted more heavily in the momentum score.

---

## Authors Field

Enter all authors as a comma-separated string:

```
Dr. John Doe, Dr. Jane Smith, Prof. Alan Turing
```

The system splits this into an array and displays each author separately on your public profile.

---

## DOI Links

If you provide a DOI, the system automatically generates a clickable link in the format `https://doi.org/YOUR_DOI` on your public profile and in the published reports.

---

## Switching Between Publication Types

Click the publication type pill buttons to switch between Journal, Conference, Book, and Book Chapter. Only the fields relevant to that type are shown in the form.

---

## Editing a Publication

Click **Edit** on any publication entry. The form opens pre-filled with existing data. Make your changes and click **Update**.

---

## Deleting a Publication

Click **Delete** on any publication entry. You will be asked to confirm before the record is removed.

---

## What is Displayed

Each publication entry shows:

- Title
- Authors
- Venue (journal or conference name) or Publisher
- Year
- Page range (if provided)
- Indexing badge — SCI or Scopus (if applicable)
- DOI link and URL link (if provided)

---

## Relationship to Analytics

Publications feed into several analytics reports:

- **Publication Trends** — year-wise output by category and indexing
- **Research Domain Profiling** — publication venues contribute to domain identification
- **Faculty Research Momentum** — weighted score based on recency and indexing quality
- **Department Health Score** — total publications and SCI/Scopus ratio are key metrics

Publications are also displayed on your public faculty profile under the **Publications** tab, grouped by type.
