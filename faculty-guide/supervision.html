Thesis & Dissertations

The Thesis & Dissertations tab lets you record your research supervision activity — PhD thesis supervisions and PG dissertation supervisions. Each has its own sub-tab.

Sub-tabs​

Sub-tabDescription
Thesis SupervisionsPhD thesis supervision records
Dissertation SupervisionsPG (Master's level) dissertation supervision records

Adding a Thesis Supervision​

  1. Select the Thesis Supervisions sub-tab
  2. Click + Add Record
  3. Fill in the details:
FieldDescriptionRequired
Thesis TitleFull title of the thesisYes
Research AreaPrimary research area, e.g. Machine Learning, VLSI DesignYes
YearYear of submission or awardYes
StatusOngoing / Completed / Submitted / AwardedYes
Your RoleSupervisor / Co-Supervisor / External ExaminerYes
  1. Click Add

Adding a Dissertation Supervision​

  1. Select the Dissertation Supervisions sub-tab
  2. Click + Add Record
  3. Fill in the details:
FieldDescriptionRequired
Dissertation TitleFull title of the dissertationYes
SpecializationSpecialization area, e.g. Data Science, Power SystemsYes
YearYear of completionYes
Your RoleSupervisor / Co-Supervisor / External ExaminerYes
  1. Click Add

Supervision Roles​

RoleDescription
SupervisorPrimary guide for the research
Co-SupervisorJoint guide alongside a primary supervisor
External ExaminerExternal evaluator for the thesis or dissertation

Thesis Status Values​

StatusMeaning
OngoingResearch is currently in progress
SubmittedThesis has been submitted for evaluation
CompletedEvaluation complete, result awaited
AwardedDegree has been formally awarded

Status badges are color-coded:

  • 🟢 Green — Completed or Awarded
  • 🟠 Orange — Ongoing
  • 🔵 Blue — Submitted

Editing a Record​

Click Edit on any supervision record. The form opens pre-filled with existing data. Make your changes and click Update.

Deleting a Record​

Click Delete on any supervision record. You will be asked to confirm before the record is removed.

What is Displayed​

Thesis record​

  • Title
  • Research area
  • Year
  • Role badge
  • Status badge (color-coded)

Dissertation record​

  • Title
  • Specialization
  • Year
  • Role badge

Relationship to Analytics​

Supervision records feed into the Supervision Pipeline analytics report, which shows:

  • How many theses are ongoing, submitted, completed, or awarded per department
  • Top supervisors by total supervision count
  • Active supervision load per faculty

Thesis supervisions are also shown on your public faculty profile if you have any records — displayed under the Thesis tab on your public page.
